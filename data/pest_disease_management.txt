INTEGRATED PEST MANAGEMENT (IPM) — Punjab Crops
=================================================

WHEAT DISEASES & PESTS
-----------------------
Yellow Rust (Stripe Rust) — Puccinia striiformis:
- Symptoms: Yellow-orange stripes along leaf veins, powdery pustules
- Favorable: Cool (10-15°C), humid weather, March-April
- Control: Propiconazole 25% EC 1ml/L OR Tebuconazole 25.9% EC 1ml/L
- Spray at first sign, repeat after 15 days if needed
- Resistant varieties: HD-3086, DBW-187

Brown Rust — Puccinia triticina:
- Symptoms: Round orange-brown pustules on upper leaf surface
- Control: Mancozeb 75% WP 2g/L OR Propiconazole 1ml/L

Karnal Bunt — Tilletia indica:
- Symptoms: Black powder (spores) replacing part of grain
- Prevention: Seed treatment with Carboxin 75% WP 2g/kg seed
- Use certified disease-free seed

Aphids (Sitobion avenae):
- Symptoms: Colonies on leaves/ears, honeydew, sooty mold
- Control: Imidacloprid 17.8% SL 0.5ml/L OR Dimethoate 30% EC 1ml/L
- Spray only if > 5 aphids/tiller

RICE/PADDY PESTS
-----------------
Stem Borer (Scirpophaga incertulas):
- Symptoms: Dead hearts (vegetative), white ears (reproductive)
- Control: Chlorpyrifos 20% EC 2L/ha OR Cartap 4G 18 kg/ha
- Pheromone traps: 5/ha for monitoring

Brown Plant Hopper (BPH):
- Symptoms: Hopperburn — circular patches of brown dead plants
- Control: Buprofezin 25% SC 1ml/L OR Pymetrozine 50% WG 0.3g/L
- AVOID: Cypermethrin, Deltamethrin (cause BPH resurgence)
- Drain field for 3-4 days to reduce humidity

Leaf Folder:
- Symptoms: Folded leaves with white papery streaks
- Control: Chlorpyrifos 20% EC 1.5L/ha (only if > 2 leaves/tiller affected)

Blast (Pyricularia oryzae):
- Symptoms: Diamond-shaped gray lesions with brown border
- Favorable: Cool nights, heavy dew, high N application
- Control: Tricyclazole 75% WP 0.6g/L OR Isoprothiolane 40% EC 1.5ml/L
- Avoid excess N fertilizer

COTTON PESTS
-------------
Whitefly (Bemisia tabaci):
- Major pest, transmits Cotton Leaf Curl Virus (CLCuV)
- Control: Spiromesifen 22.9% SC 1ml/L OR Diafenthiuron 50% WP 1g/L
- Yellow sticky traps: 10/acre for monitoring
- Avoid: Imidacloprid, Thiamethoxam (resistance developed)

Pink Bollworm (Pectinophora gossypiella):
- Symptoms: Rosette flowers, damaged bolls with pink larvae
- Control: Emamectin benzoate 5% SG 0.4g/L
- Pheromone traps: 5/acre

Thrips (Thrips tabaci):
- Symptoms: Silvery streaks on leaves, upward curling
- Control: Fipronil 5% SC 1.5ml/L OR Spinosad 45% SC 0.3ml/L

GENERAL IPM PRINCIPLES
------------------------
1. Monitor fields twice weekly — early detection saves cost
2. Use Economic Threshold Level (ETL) before spraying — don't spray prophylactically
3. Prefer biological control: Trichogramma cards for stem borer (50,000/acre)
4. Rotate pesticide groups to prevent resistance
5. Spray in early morning or evening — avoid hot afternoon
6. Use proper PPE: gloves, mask, goggles when spraying
7. Never mix pesticides without checking compatibility
8. Dispose empty containers safely — never near water bodies
