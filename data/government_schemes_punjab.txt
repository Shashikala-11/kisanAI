GOVERNMENT SCHEMES FOR PUNJAB FARMERS
=======================================

PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi):
- ₹6,000/year direct benefit transfer in 3 installments of ₹2,000
- Eligibility: All small/marginal farmers with cultivable land
- Register: pmkisan.gov.in or nearest CSC center
- Documents: Aadhaar, bank account, land records (Jamabandi)

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) — Crop Insurance:
- Premium: 1.5% for Rabi crops (wheat), 2% for Kharif crops (rice, cotton)
- Covers: Natural calamities, pest/disease, post-harvest losses
- Enroll: Before sowing through bank or CSC
- Claim: Within 72 hours of crop loss via app or bank

Punjab Kisan Credit Card (KCC):
- Short-term crop loan at 4% interest (after 3% subvention)
- Limit: Based on land holding and crop
- Apply: Any nationalized bank or cooperative bank
- Includes personal accident insurance ₹50,000

Mera Pani Meri Virasat (Punjab):
- Incentive: ₹7,000/acre for farmers who shift from paddy to other crops
- Eligible crops: Maize, cotton, vegetables, pulses, oilseeds
- Condition: Must not grow paddy in that field
- Apply: Agriculture department portal

Parali (Paddy Straw) Management Scheme:
- Subsidy: 50-80% on Happy Seeder, Super SMS, Rotavator, Paddy Straw Chopper
- Individual farmer: 50% subsidy
- FPO/Cooperative: 80% subsidy
- Apply: agrimachinerypb.com

PM Kisan Mandhan Yojana (Pension):
- Monthly pension ₹3,000 after age 60
- Contribution: ₹55-200/month (age-based)
- Eligibility: Small/marginal farmers aged 18-40

Soil Health Card Scheme:
- Free soil testing every 2 years
- Personalized fertilizer recommendations
- Contact: Punjab Agriculture Department or KVK

e-NAM (National Agriculture Market):
- Online trading platform for mandi prices
- Register at enam.gov.in
- Get better prices by accessing buyers across India
