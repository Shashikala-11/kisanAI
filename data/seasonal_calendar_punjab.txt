PUNJAB CROP CALENDAR & SEASONAL GUIDE
=======================================

KHARIF SEASON (June – November)
---------------------------------
June:
- Transplant paddy (after June 10 — legal requirement)
- Sow cotton (April-May ideal, June last chance)
- Sow maize (last week June – first week July)
- Sow moong/mash (June 15 – July 15)
- Apply basal fertilizer before transplanting/sowing
- Prepare nursery for paddy (May 15 – June 5)

July:
- Complete paddy transplanting by July 10
- First irrigation to cotton after 3-4 weeks
- Monitor for stem borer in paddy
- Apply first top-dress N to paddy (21 DAT)
- Weed management in all Kharif crops

August:
- Apply second top-dress N to paddy (42 DAT)
- Monitor BPH in paddy — peak season
- Cotton: Monitor whitefly, apply if ETL crossed
- Maize: Apply N at knee-high stage

September:
- Paddy: Panicle initiation — critical irrigation stage
- Cotton: Boll development — maintain moisture
- Maize: Tasseling — apply last N dose
- Start planning Rabi crop inputs

October:
- Harvest early paddy varieties (PR-126) from Oct 1
- Harvest maize
- Prepare wheat fields — laser leveling, deep plowing
- Apply FYM/compost for wheat
- Harvest late paddy (Pusa-44) by Oct 31

November:
- SOW WHEAT: Nov 1–25 (optimal Nov 1–15)
- Apply basal fertilizer for wheat
- Sow mustard (Oct 15 – Nov 15)
- Sow potato (Oct 15 – Nov 15 in plains)
- Sow vegetables: Peas, cauliflower, cabbage

RABI SEASON (November – April)
--------------------------------
December:
- First irrigation to wheat (CRI stage, 20-21 DAS) — CRITICAL
- Apply first top-dress N after first irrigation
- Monitor for yellow rust in wheat
- Irrigate mustard at flowering

January:
- Second irrigation to wheat (tillering, 40-45 DAS)
- Apply second top-dress N
- Harvest early potato
- Sow spring maize (Jan 15 – Feb 15)

February:
- Third irrigation to wheat (jointing, 60-65 DAS)
- Monitor wheat for aphids and rust
- Harvest mustard
- Sow sunflower (Feb 1–28)

March:
- Fourth irrigation to wheat (flowering, 80-85 DAS)
- Spray fungicide if rust appears
- Harvest potato
- Prepare cotton fields

April:
- Fifth irrigation to wheat (grain filling, 100-105 DAS)
- Harvest wheat: April 15 – May 10
- Sow cotton: April 15 – May 15
- Prepare paddy nursery beds (May 15 onwards)

ZAID/SUMMER SEASON (March – June)
-----------------------------------
- Sunflower: Feb–June
- Spring maize: Jan–May
- Vegetables: Cucumber, bitter gourd, bottle gourd
- Mung bean: March–June (short duration, fits between wheat and paddy)
- Watermelon/Muskmelon: Feb–May

IMPORTANT DATES TO REMEMBER:
- June 10: Earliest paddy transplanting date (Punjab law)
- Nov 1: Start of optimal wheat sowing window
- Nov 25: Last date for optimal wheat sowing
- April 15: Start wheat harvest / Start cotton sowing
