FERTILIZER & SOIL HEALTH — Punjab
====================================

Punjab Soil Health Status (based on Soil Health Card data):
- 80% soils deficient in organic carbon (< 0.5%)
- 60% soils deficient in zinc
- 40% soils deficient in sulphur
- Most soils: medium N, low to medium P, medium to high K
- pH: 7.5–8.5 (alkaline) in most districts

Soil Health Card Scheme:
- Free soil testing at government labs
- Contact: Punjab Agriculture Department, nearest KVK
- Test every 2 years for best results
- Tests: N, P, K, pH, EC, OC, S, Zn, Fe, Mn, Cu, B

Urea (46% N):
- Most common N fertilizer
- Use neem-coated urea (NCU) — mandatory for retail sale
- NCU reduces N loss by 10-15%, increases yield by 5-6%
- Apply in 2-3 splits, never apply all at once
- Do not apply before rain (leaching loss)

DAP (Diammonium Phosphate, 18% N + 46% P2O5):
- Best basal fertilizer for wheat and rice
- Apply at sowing/transplanting
- Price: ~₹1,350/bag (50 kg) — subsidized

MOP (Muriate of Potash, 60% K2O):
- Apply as basal dose
- Important for cotton and potato
- Price: ~₹850/bag (50 kg)

Zinc Sulphate (ZnSO4):
- 21% Zn (heptahydrate) or 33% Zn (monohydrate)
- Apply 25 kg/ha for deficient soils
- Soil application every 2-3 years OR foliar spray 0.5% solution
- Critical for rice, wheat, maize

Organic Farming:
- FYM (Farm Yard Manure): Apply 10 tonnes/ha, incorporate 3-4 weeks before sowing
- Vermicompost: 2-3 tonnes/ha, better nutrient availability
- Green manure: Dhaincha (Sesbania) — sow in June, incorporate before rice transplanting
- Biofertilizers: Azotobacter (wheat/maize), Rhizobium (legumes), PSB (all crops)
  Save 20-25% chemical N, improve soil health

Crop Residue Management:
- NEVER burn paddy straw — causes air pollution, kills soil microbes
- Happy Seeder: Sow wheat directly in paddy stubble — saves ₹3,000-4,000/ha
- Paddy straw composting: Mix with urea + water, ready in 25-30 days
- Subsidies available for Happy Seeder, Super SMS, Rotavator under Punjab scheme

Integrated Nutrient Management (INM):
- Combine 50% chemical + 50% organic for best results
- Reduces input cost by 20-25%
- Improves soil organic carbon over time
